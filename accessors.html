Geometry.boundary()¶
Returns:closure
Return type:Geometry

Returns the closure of the combinatorial boundary of this geometric object. Because the result of this function is a closure, and hence topologically closed, the resulting boundary can be represented using representational Geometry primitives.

This method implements OpenGIS® Simple Feature Access specification. OGC 06-103r4 6.1.2.2.

tarantool> gis.Polygon({ {{10, 130}, {50, 190}, {110, 190}, {140, 150}, {150, 80}, {100, 10}, {20, 40}, {10, 130}},
         > {{70, 40}, {100, 50}, {120, 80}, {80, 110}, {50, 90}, {70, 40}} }, 0):boundary()
---
- MULTILINESTRING ((10 130, 50 190, 110 190, 140 150, 150 80, 100 10, 20 40, 10 130),
  (70 40, 100 50, 120 80, 80 110, 50 90, 70 40))
...
